Katherine Cass
I'm one of the organizers. I attended GHC last year and it changed my life. I met new heros, got an internship, and fell in love with my new major. I loved it so much I'm making sure each and every one of us is able to attend this year.
Jessie Adkins
I met Bonnie Ross, manager of the company that produces Halo. It's like meeting a celebrity. People asked her why there are so few women in game development. She asked them, 'Why aren't you in the industry? Why aren't you making games?' It was the first time anybody told me that I could.
Haley Eisenshtadt
I never thought I would find as many female role models in technology as I did at last year's conference. I met my future Microsoft Studios supervisor at the conference, connected with Case alumni, discovered the true breadth of the fields of technology, and gained confidence.
Katie Pezzot
I'm a senior computer science and statistics major from Columbus. I want to attend Grace Hopper to learn more about a variety of computing topics (especially data science) and to meet other women around the country interested in computing.
Dina Benayad-Cherif
